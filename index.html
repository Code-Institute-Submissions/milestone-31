ABOUT US...

Elite Gourmet was founded in the Spring of 2020. The concept sprung from the vibrant food and hospitality scene in the Cork region. There was a continuous rising tide of excellence among the various eateries in and around the city. As this was also reflected across the country, and with more Michelin starred establishments appearing every year, there was a need for a conduit towards this culinary renaissance. We made real connections with the best quality venues across the whole island of Ireland, and seek to organise the perfect dining experience for the decerning customer, be they local or a vistor to these shores.
